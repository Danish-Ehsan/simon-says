How to play ▼

Press the Play button to begin. The objective of the game is to repeat the pattern of button presses shown to you at the start of each round. Each time you succeed in replicating the pattern, an extra step is added to the end of the pattern. Beat the game by completing the length of the pattern defined by the win limit.

Modes ▼

Standard:
Press the Play button to begin. In standard mode, if you fail to repeat the pattern, the pattern will be played again and then you will have another chance to replicate the pattern.

Strict:
Activate at any time by pressing the Strict button. In strict mode if you fail to repeat the pattern the game will end and you will have to start over from the beginning.

Random:
Activated by pressing the Rnd button. In Random mode, each round will contain a new randomly generated pattern. The length of the random pattern will increase by 1 after each successful round.

Change Win Limit ▼

In order to change the win limit, hold the Play button until the button and the win limit number begins to blink. Use the Strict and Rnd buttons to increase or decrease the win limit respectively. Press the Play button again to save the win limit and begin a new game.
